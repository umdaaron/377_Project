About GradJobs

GradJobs is a platform designed to help graduates find job opportunities in their fields of interest, particularly within the federal sector.

We utilize APIs like USAJOBS and Open Skills to connect users with relevant job postings.


Collaborators: Aaron Tekle, Amina Shabbir, Hassan Wasim, Richard Lac, and Kedrala Mohammed
